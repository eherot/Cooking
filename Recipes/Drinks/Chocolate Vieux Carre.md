# Chocolate Vieux Carré

3/4 ounce rye\
3/4 ounce brandy\
3/4 ounce sweet vermouth\
1/4 ounce Bénédictine\
2 dashes orange bitters\
1 tsp chocolate extract

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice and stir until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a twist of orange peel, if desired. Enjoy your Chocolate Vieux Carré!
