# Houska

(2 Houskas)

## Ingredients

|amount|ingredient|
|-|-|
|14 g| dry yeast|
|175 mL| warm water|
|235 mL| milk|
|115 g| sugar|
|57 g (1/2 stick)| butter|
|1 1/2 tsp| salt|
|637 g| flour|
|3.8 g (1 tsp)| lemon zest|
|3 eggs|(1 for icing w/conf. sugar & van.)|
|175 g| candied fruit (about half citrus peel, half kiwi + strawberry + ginger)|
|80 g|whole almonds|
|113 g| candied cherries (1 pkg.)|

## Directions

**Mix and set aside:** yeast with water

**In pan:** Scald milk. Add sugar, butter, salt. Cool.

**Add to warm mixture:** 283 g flour, lemon zest and 2 eggs. Beat well. Stir in yeast mixture and candied fruit. Add remaining 354 g flour. Cover for 10 minutes.

Turn out to knead gently (just to mix). Put into greased bowl to let rise until double (abuot 1 hour) Shape into braids. Put on greased sheet. Let rise til double. Stick in almonds and brush with egg yolk and water mixture. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes. Frost with egg white, confectioner's sugar and vanilla. Top with cherries.
