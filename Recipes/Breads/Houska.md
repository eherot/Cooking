# Houska

(2 Houskas)

2 pkg dry yeast
3/4 C warm water
1 C milk
1/2 C sugar
1/4 C butter
1 1/2 tsp salt
4 1/2 C flour
1 tsp grated lemon ride
3 eggs (1 for icing w/conf. sugar & van.)
1 C candied fruit
whole blanched almonds
1 pkg. candied cherries

<ins>Mix and set aside:</ins> yeast with water <ins>In pan:</ins> Scald milk. Add sugar, butter, salt. Cool. Add to warm mixture, 2 C flour, lemon rind and 2 eggs. Beat well. Stir in yeast mixture and candied fruit. Add 2 1/2 C flour. Cover for 10 minutes and turn out to knead gently (just to mix). Put into greased bowl to let rise until double (abuot 1 hour) Shape into braids. Put on greased sheet. Let rise til double. Stick in almonds and brush with egg yolk and water mixture. Bake at 350 F for 25 minutes. Frost with egg white, confectioner's sugar and vanilla. Top with cherries.
