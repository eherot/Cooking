Rolls:
2 cups + 2 tbsp cake flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp cold butter cut into 1/2 inch cubes
3/8 cup whole milk

Filling:
2 tbsp butter, melted
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Frosting:
1 oz cream cheese, softened
1 tbsp butter, melted
3/8 cup confectioners sugar
1/2 tsp milk
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Preheat oven to 425

Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt

Cut butter into flour.

Add milk and stir until it forms a ball

Turn dough onto well-floured surface and pat into a rough rectangle 1" thick.  Fold over and pat down again (gently).  Repeat a third time.  Gently pat dough to make a 10" x 3" strip.

Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon

Brush each strip with melted butter and sprinkle with brown sugar + cinnamon mixture.

Roll the strip into a 3" wide log and slice into three 1" biscuits.

Place in a bread pan,  top with any remaining melted butter

Bake for 10 to 15 minutes.

Meanwhile, prepare the frosting by mixing together all of the frosting ingredients.  Pour onto the biscuits once they've cooled for five minutes.
